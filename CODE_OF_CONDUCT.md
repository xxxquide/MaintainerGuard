# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in MaintainerGuard respectful and welcoming,
regardless of experience level, identity, background, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish secrets or private repository data.
- Avoid security fearmongering and unsupported claims.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and project direction.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and
publication of others' private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict
participation when needed to protect the community. Report conduct concerns
privately to the repository maintainers.
